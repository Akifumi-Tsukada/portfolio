生い立ち

1992年東京都府中市生まれ。生後まもなく両親の離婚により北海道に移り住み大学入学まで函館市で育つ。
地元の名門校函館ラ・サール高校に通うも周囲と馴染めず、インターネットに没頭し成績が落ち込む。成績の落ち込みにつれ、学業成績にしか興味を示さなかった母親しか家庭内で関わりがなかったため孤立する。
高校卒業後は、インターネットに可能性を感じて2年間ひきこもりになり、チャットサイトのメンバーと交流し自らの居場所を作り上げていく。
しかし、チャットサイトで交流する仲間も各自の事情などにより失っていくと、大学に入学することを決意する。
地元の私立大学に入学するとボランティアサークル、NPOでの活動に社会的意義を感じボランティア活動に打ち込む。
卒業後は社会課題を解決する仕事を志望するも就職先が見つからず、1年間フリーターとして生活する。
その後、上京しインターネットに可能性を感じていた興味からIT業界に3年ほど勤務する。

現在は自分と同じような孤独に追い込まれる人々が増加する社会課題を解決するための仕事に転職、または起業を目指す。
将来的には根本からの社会課題解決のため政界への参加を目指す。

AkifumiTsukada
Born in Fuchu, Tokyo in 1992, he moved to Hokkaido shortly after his parents divorced and grew up in Hakodate until he entered university.
Although high school attended the prestigious local school Hakodate La Salle, he does not get used to the surroundings and is absorbed in the Internet and his grades drop. Since the family has only him and his mother who is only interested in academic performance, he became isolated from his family without any understanding when his grades declined.
After graduating from high school, he felt the potential of the Internet and became a hikikomori for two years, interacting with members of the chat site and creating his own place.
However, he decides to enroll in college when he lost his friends on her chat site due to their own circumstances.
When he enrolls in a local private university, he feels the social significance of his activities at volunteer circles and NPOs and devotes hisself to volunteer activities.
After graduating, he wants to work to solve social issues, but he cannot find a job and lives as a part-time worker for a year.
After that, he moved to Tokyo and worked in the IT industry for about three years because of his interest in the Internet.

I am aiming to change jobs or start a business to solve social issues.
In the future, I would like to participate in politics in order to solve social issues from the ground up.
